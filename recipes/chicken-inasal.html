Baked Chicken Inasal Recipe

Description

Chicken inasal is a flavorful chicken dish from the Philippines that is usually cooked on a grill and served with rice and a dipping sauce. This recipe will show you how to make this tasty chicken dish in an oven.

Ingredients

Chicken and the Marinade

  • 2 lbs chicken, with skin attached
  • 1 cup spiced vinegar
  • 1 2/3 tbsp brown sugar
    • 3/4 cup lemongrass, chopped
  • 2 inch peeled ginger, sliced and crushed
  • 1 whole garlic, peeled and crushed
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice or calamansi juice
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper

For Basting

  • 1/4 cup annatto oil or 1/4 cup olive oil mixed with 1 1/2 tsps annatto powder
  • 2 tbsps butter, melted
  • 2 tsps salt
  • 2 tbsps lemon juice or calamansi juice

For the Dipping Sauce

  • Spiced vinegar or mixture of spiced vinegar and soy sauce

Steps

  1. Mix the chicken and marinade ingredients in a large Pyrex bowl or a freezer bag, such as Ziploc. Massage the marinade into the chicken and cover the bowl with plastic wrap (or remove the air if using a Ziploc bag) and let marinate in the refrigerator overnight or at least 6 hours.
  2. Mix the ingredients for the basting sauce in a separate bowl. Set aside.
  3. Preheat cast iron pan and add a little annatto oil. Take the chicken from the refrigerator and sear both sides on the pan on medium-high heat until browned, about 4 minutes.
  4. While searing the chicken, start preheating the oven to 400F. Transfer the chicken to a nonstick baking tray and bake for 40 minutes, until the thickest part is 165F inside. During the last 15 minutes, baste the chicken every 5 minutes.
  5. Serve the chicken with rice and dipping sauce.
